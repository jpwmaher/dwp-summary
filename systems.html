Glossary of systems

  • Summary Care Record application
  • GP Connect
  • National Record Locator (NRL)
  • Local health and care record (LHCR)
01.

Summary Care Record application (SCRa)


Used by:

  • paramedics for accessing medical information needed in an emergency
  • community pharmacy to help prescribe medicines
  • data quality teams to correct data errors in other systems

14+ use cases identified and being investigated by NHS Digital

02.

GP Connect


Used by:

  • GP’ (who isn’t the users registered GP) to view a patient’s GP practice record
  • a pharmacist to access the GP practice record and update it with structured medical information
  • GP’s who are transferring a record from a previous patients practice

03.

National Record Locator (NRL)


Enables authorised users to find specific patient records that are held on different healthcare systems. It does this by acting as a registry by pointing (or bookmarking) to show a user, that a patient record exists and where it is held.

Eventually the aim will be to provide access to the records that have been located.


04.

Local health and care record (LHCR)


Local health and care records are regional held records consuming:

  • GP records
  • hospital records
  • potentially user submitted medical information eg. from smartwatches

This program is currently rolling out in 7 regions.
