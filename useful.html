What is useful?

We’ve spoken to Health Care Professionals and Decision Makers during to determine what data is useful to them in decision making. Our research has found that useful data is very subjective depending on the claimants condition and the particular benefit they’re claiming.

There is no one answer to what data is useful, this is proven out over the last 3 years of work carried out by DWP in the health space as well as our own research.

We believe that the data items highlighted in the technically possible section for the various systems are what are regarded as helpful data items for determining someone's eligibility for payment, referral to an assessment or disallowance.
